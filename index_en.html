MAJESTIC COUNTRY HOUSE MAKES FOR AN UNFORGETTABLE STAY IN CHARLEVOIX

Whether you're hosting a family reunion, business event or gathering with friends, La belle d’Alfred-Pellan is the perfect venue in Petite-Rivière-Saint-François. This three-storey contemporary showplace combines the comfort of a deluxe residence with the charm of a secluded cottage in the woods. Enjoy stunning views of the St. Lawrence River and Massif de Charlevoix from the huge open-concept living/dining room. Sleeping up to 16 guests, the house features five bedrooms - four with ensuite - and offers a sweeping upper-level balcony. La belle d’Alfred-Pellan is equipped with everything you need for a memorable stay: two living rooms with smart TV, two gas fireplaces, pool table, foosball table and other games, plus an outdoor hot tub and fire pit.
